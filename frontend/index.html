What is CrawlConda

CrawlConda is an AI fact-checking system. Give it any claim — it searches 24 live RSS feeds across world news, business, tech, science, and sport, runs a 4-node AI pipeline, and returns a source-grounded verdict.

Every verdict is pinned permanently to IPFS. Humans can confirm or challenge any verdict — votes are stored alongside the AI verdict forever.

How It Works

1
Expander — converts your claim into optimised RSS search keywords
2
Searcher — scans all 24 feeds + Google News, ranks by relevance, returns top 8 articles
3
Scanner — extracts only facts explicitly stated in sources, no outside knowledge
4
Verdict — strict source-grounded verdict with exact headline citation
